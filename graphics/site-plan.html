Overview

Purpose

The purpose statement of this graphic design portfolio website is to showcase my creativity, skills, and experience in graphic design to potential clients or employers. It should highlight my unique style and demonstrate my ability to solve design problems effectively.

Audience

The audience of this graphic design portfolio website are potential clients looking to hire a graphic designer, employers seeking to fill design positions, or even fellow designers and artists who are interested in my work.
